A. Living Accommodation

This is a private agreement with the sole purpose of personal usage of an accommodation in a determined living space. This is not intended to be used for personal procedures in public or private Databases neither related to permanent residency of any kind since the tenant is only requesting a temporary accommodation and will be granted with just that.

Article 1. Renter lets out for personal use a room on the apartment in: H. Cleyndertweg, Amsterdam Noord. This includes also the use of the kitchen, shower and toilet.

Article 2. The agreement has been made for the period from - to -. The tenant is responsible for paying the rent during this entire period. In case the tenant wants to move out before the end of the contract, the deposit will not be refunded. The Renter can give immediate notice to the tenant to move out in case of any trouble.

B. Rent and Related Living Costs

Article 3. The rent of the room is 1,390 Euros per month. Which includes the cost of gas, electricity, water, Internet and furniture in the room.

Article 4. Security Deposit: 2,000 Euros. Refunded after end of contract if no damage. Refund may take up to 30 days. If tenant moves out early, deposit is not refunded. Booking amount: 500 Euros (non-refundable reservation).

Article 5. Rent paid in advance on the 30th of every month. Penalty of 50 EUR per day of delay.

C. Obligations

Article 6. Renter provides room in clean state with no defects.

Article 7. Tenant must not cause trouble to neighbors or roommates. Must share cleaning. Agrees to security cameras. No subletting, no pets, no overnight guests. Room must be returned clean.

D. End of Agreement

Article 8. Tenant is responsible for paying rent on time during the full period.
